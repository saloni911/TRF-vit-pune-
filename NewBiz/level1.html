TRF Level 2 Workshop

  1. Home

From Workshop to Robotics Community: Skills, Projects, and Careers.

The Level 2 Workshop by The Robotics Forum is dedicated to in-depth knowledge sharing through theory lectures and practical sessions in the domains of Mechanical, Electronics and Programming. This workshop opens the doors for the participants to be a technical team member of The Robotics Forum. The participants are tested through the rigorous training of 60 days and then recruited after a number of evaluations. There are many guest lectures arranged for the participants in which the alumni and professors guide them for the future journey.

A community of tech enthusiasts for learning, sharing and innovation. Future ticks with our robots!
