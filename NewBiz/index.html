THE ROBOTICS FORUM

The Robotics Forum is BRACT’s VIT Pune's presiding robotics organization, with a 17-year legacy. The diverse community of TRF is made up of students from various engineering disciplines that are passionate about robotics and automation.

Know More

About Us

TRF is VIT Pune's foremost and leading robotics club with a 17-year legacy. Founded by a group of robotics enthusiasts, the organization has participated in and won many national level robotics competitions and hackathons. A key goal of The Robotics Forum is to inspire a passion for robotics in students, in addition to providing them with an opportunity to showcase their dynamic, creative and knowledge skills.

ABU ROBOCON

The ABU Asia-Pacific Robot Contest (ABU Robocon) is an Asian-Oceanian college robot competition, founded in 2002 by Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union. In the competition robots compete to complete a task within a set period of time.

Eyantra

e-Yantra is a robotics outreach program funded by the Ministry of Education and hosted at IIT Bombay. The goal is to harness the talent of young engineers to solve problems using technology across a variety of domains such as: agriculture, manufacturing, defence, home, smart-city maintenance and service industries.

Smart India Hackathon (SIH)

Smart India Hackathon 2023 is a nationwide initiative to provide students a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem solving.

RoboVision

Giving vision to our robots, this Workshop empowers participants with Linux and ROS proficiency, utilizing the capabilities of image processing and computer vision to equip robots for sight-based challenges.

Roboroid

Level 1

The level 1 workshop is a flagship workshop by The Robotics Forum for the first year students. This workshop "Learning by Doing" the workshop includes fundamentals of mechanical, electronics and programming with hands-on experience on a miniature robot.

Read more
PCB Designing

This workshop is designed for aspiring robot builders who want to master the art of Printed Circuit Board (PCB) design. The workshop includes the fundamentals of how to translate your robotic ideas into functioning circuits, unlocking a world of creative possibilities.

CAD Modelling

The CAD workshop is a comprehensive workshop covering essentials from engineering drawing fundamentals to 3D modeling of intricate parts and assemblies along with animations and simulations using Autodesk Fusion 360.

Level 2

The Level 2 workshop offers an in-depth, advanced training program for our newly recruited members. This interactive course is designed to enhance skills across key domains including mechanics, electronics, and programming, equipping participants with the expertise required for robotics development.
